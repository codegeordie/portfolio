Who I Am

I'm a front-end web developer based in Philadelphia. I make streamlined, responsive websites and applications. I work hard to create user experiences that are simple, while resonating with clean, understated design. That same ideal applies to the code I write, keeping my projects as trim and functional as possible. Below, you'll find some examples of my work which I hope you'll enjoy.

When I'm not coding, I try to be skiing, hiking, or just generally outdoors. That said, I'm always looking forward to new challenges and to creating websites that both my clients and I are proud of. I'm currently available for hire, for contract work or a full-time position. If you need a developer, I hope to hear from you soon.
